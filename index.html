About Me

A brief introduction to who I am and what drives my passion for software development.

I'm a software developer who loves building things that actually make life easier — from slick frontends to solid backends and everything in between. My coding journey started in university and quickly turned into a full-blown passion for solving real-world problems with digital tools (and a lot of coffee).

At first, some things seemed impossible, but the moment I started to really get it, that’s when the fun truly began — and that drive kept me learning and pushing forward. I’m all about writing code that’s clean enough to brag about and smart enough to actually work smoothly. When I’m not coding, you’ll probably catch me geeking out over the latest tech trends, contributing to open-source projects, or balancing time between hiking, hitting the gym, and playing video games — because a healthy work-life balance means mixing fresh air, sweat, and a little friendly competition!
